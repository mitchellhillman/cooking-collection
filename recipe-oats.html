Overnight Oats

Preparation

Combine oats, milk, maple syrup and vanilla in a mason jar or bowl. Seal with a lid and shake to mix, or stir if using a bowl. (If making a larger batch, you can stir the ingredients together in a bowl.) Refrigerate at least 6 hours and up to 4 days. Stir and add toppings right before serving.

Ingredients

Qty Item
1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp maple syrup
1/4 tsp vanilla
2 Tbsp dried fruits or nuts
